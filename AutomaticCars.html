Automatic Cars

An automatic car is a car that changes gears by itself. You don’t need to press the clutch or shift gears manually. You just press the accelerator to go and the brake to stop.

Key Features of an Automatic Car

    No clutch pedal
    Easy to drive in traffic
    Gears change automatically
    Smoother driving experience
    Good for beginners
