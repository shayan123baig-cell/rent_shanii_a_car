Hybrid Cars

Hybrid cars are vehicles that use two types of power:
    1. Petrol/Diesel engine
    2. Electric motor (battery)
Both work together to give better mileage and less fuel cost.
    At low speed, the car uses the electric motor → saves fuel.
    At high speed, the engine + electric motor both work.

⭐ Benefits of Hybrid Cars

    Better fuel average
    Less pollution
    Smooth driving
    Lower fuel cost
    Quiet engine sound
